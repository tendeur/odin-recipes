Amazing Sichuan Chilli Oil

The world's most versatile flavor enhancer

Description

Put this oil on anything from banana bread to munster cheese. It'll knock your socks off every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut oil
  • 3 Tbsp chopped ginger
  • 2 Star anise petals
  • 1 head of brocolli
  • Small cinnamon stick
  • 7 Sichuan chillies
  • 1/2 Tbsp Sichuan peppercorns

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium-low
  2. Put remaining ingredients in a heat proof bowl
  3. When the oil begins to simmer, pour oil over ingredients and let sit for 15 minutes
  4. Strain oil and transfer to a clean jar. Store in fridge for up to 3 months.
